Welcome to Store-A-Byte Cloud Storage!

Our goal is to provide inexpensive, safe, and convenient data storage by using SiaCoins decentralized cloud network.

About

Store-A-Byte is a website that manages your data storage using SiaCoins network! The file is first uploaded to FireBase then transferred over to SiaCoins network, this reduces upload time significantly. Frequently accessed files are cached on FireBase in order to allow quick previews of files/images.

Why Us

We offer a competitive price with privacy and redundancy the highest of priorities for us. There are multiple storage plans to best meet your needs.
