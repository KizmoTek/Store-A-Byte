Store-A-Byte

Welcome to Store-A-Byte Cloud Storage!

Our goal is to provide inexpensive, safe, and convenient data storage by using SiaCoins decentralized cloud network.

About

Store-A-Byte is a website that manages your data storage using the SiaCoin network. To store a file, we first upload it to FireBase, and then transfer over to the SiaCoin network. Decentralized cloud storage takes a long time to store data, and is not very user-friendly. In order to make saving data quick and convenient for customers, we first store it on FireBase. This not only reduces upload time significantly, but also saves the user frustrating download errors.

Why Us

Our highest priorities are price, redundancy, and privacy, which we believe are the most important qualities to our customers. There are multiple storage plans to best meet your needs.
