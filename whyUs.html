Safety

We use a decentralized cloud storage network to store data. What this means is that data stored through our website will be encrypted, and sent to computers around the world. This ensures that your data will not be lost.

Price

Centralized cloud storage services such as Amazon Web Services, ICloud, and Microsoft Azure store data in large facilities which require protection, maintenance, and air conditioning. Decentralized cloud storage stores data on the hard drives of volunteers around the world. Data can be stored much more cheaply this way. Additionally, Store-A-Byte is built for the average consumer, who doesn't need complicated features such as those provided by AWS, Wasabi, and Microsoft Azure. This allows us to lower costs for our customers even further.
